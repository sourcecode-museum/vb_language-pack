VB Language Pack Generator
 

1. Updating a Language Pack

If you created a Language Pack and, let's say, updated your Project, with new objects or new forms for example, you can udpate the pack too. Just open the existing Language Pack then select your Visual Basic Project and click the "Load VBP" button, so the Generator will automatically add new objects and forms to your pack.

NOTE: Don't forget to select a new "Output File" or the same you opened and click "Generate Pack" to save.
