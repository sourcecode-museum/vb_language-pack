1. Editing in Grid View

To edit captions and tooltips with a Grid View you need to click in a form listed or in the "Strings" ítem in the "Project list" at the left, then right-click in it. You will see a Popup menu, just click "Grid View". All objects of the selected form will be listed in the "Grid View" window, you can edit all captions and tooltips in this window. It's very easy and fast to translate a form in grid view.

NOTE: Don't forget to click "Save" when you finish editing the grid or you will lost what you changed.
