VB Language Pack Generator
 

1. Editing a Language Pack

To edit an existing Language Pack just click the "Open Pack" button. You will see everything you saw when you loaded the Visual Basic Project but with all information you changed, like translated captions and tooltips.

Just change whatever you want, set a new "Output File" or the same then click in "Generate Pack" to save.
